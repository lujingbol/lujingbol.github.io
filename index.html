Bio

Jingbo Lu is a Research Associate supervised by Prof. Jingling Xue at School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of New South Wales (UNSW). He received B.Sc degree in Physics from Nankai University (NKU) in 2013. He received M.Eng degree in Information Technology from University of New South Wales(UNSW) in 2016. He received Ph.D. degree in 2020.

His areas of interest are compilers and programming languages. In particular, his current research focuses on static program analysis. He has great interest in developing fundamental static and dynamic program analysis techniques and tools to improve the usability, reliability and security of complicated software systems.
