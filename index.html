Bio

Jingbo Lu is working at Ant Group as a Senior Engineer. Before joining Ant, he worked as a Principle Scientist in SAST Research Group at Sectrend Inc.. He received his M.Eng from University of New South Wales(UNSW) and Ph.D. degree from University of New South Wales(UNSW) under the supervision of Scientia Professor Jingling Xue .

Research Interests

His areas of interest are compilers and programming languages. In particular, his current research focuses on static program analysis. He has great interest in developing fundamental static and dynamic program analysis techniques and tools to improve the usability, reliability and security of complicated software systems.
